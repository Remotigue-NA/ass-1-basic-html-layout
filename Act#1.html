MAIN CONTENT

Sentinels have announced the return of Tyson ‘TenZ’ Ngo to its Valorant team’s starting lineup after the player missed three weeks of action due to illness and a finger injury. TenZ played no part in the team’s last four games in VCT Americas due to illness and a finger injury. During that time, Sentinels fielded their substitute player, Jimmy ‘Marved’ Nguyen, changing some of the team’s roles to accommodate the change. The former OpTic Gaming player helped Sentinels to its first win in the league since its opening match, but the squad did not do much beyond that and currently has a 2-5 record.

SIDE CONTENT

Sentinels crowned itself champion of the VALORANT Champions Tour 2024: Americas Kickoff last night after a back-and-forth brawl with LOUD that took all five maps to complete. As a result, Sentinels will be representing the Americas region at VCT 2024: Masters Madrid as the 1st seed, with LOUD settling for the 2nd seed as the Kickoff runner-up.
